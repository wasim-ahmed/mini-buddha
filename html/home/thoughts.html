OverThinking/Anxiety

-If you keep talking to someone else in your mind then it is called as overthinking. It is 99% bad for our health, just take a pause for one moment and think does talking to someone in your head will will lead you somewhere? the answer you must have got is NO, so why do it!. Now the truth is we indulge in overthinking often when we are afraid of confronting a situation, see this is how our mind plays with us. It let us talk away from situation but when we are present in that situation chances are it won't help!. This is the normal behaviour of an untrained mind, we just have to train our mind the same way we train our muscles to be strong.

We have to train our mind in not to indulge in overthinking, then we can face the actual scenario with more confidence. if you are talking to someone else in your mind the reason may be the other person is too adamant or ignorant, just leave it there. May be the other person is not worth or it will understand after some time. Whatever be the reason of the behaviour of other person, overthinking will harm only you and not the other person. In such scenarios take 2 minutes decide what ever you feel is correct and come out of that situation. Get yourself busy in some other activity.
