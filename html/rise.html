Do not block your mind (don't avoid your problems) rather get a clarity of what you want to do. Be engaged in your day to day activities as you were before and do not overload your brain. Things may take time to settle down but that is the law of nature everthing happnes organically it takes time.As long as you are having a calm mind nothing can hurt you.

Mind traverses between problems only like among actual problem & side problems like work, career, health etc. Inspite Mind needs rest whcih can be obtained by mindfullness only.It was a habit which was there earlier automatically but now lost over a period of time, due to bad time. Take care of your body

No need for an agreement on this one, this is the most important thing this is you. We also need to understand the difference between fitness and body building. What we need is fitness, body building and good shape comes next. The only thing reqiured to achieve great fitness is dedication and discipline. Dedication that you are going to place your fitness goal above any other thing in life and we require a great amount of Discipline to achieve that.


Discipline is of utmost importance!

Your health is dependent on your lifestyle & habits, what you eat, when you eat, how much you eat. When you sleep, how much do you sleep. How much brain you utilize, how much physical exercise you do. How well are your body cycles stablished. and at last how much stress do you have.

Every voluntary action is subjected to addiction,identify & control it. Stop any kind of addiction it can be

-Spending too much time on social media like facebook,youtube etc

practise simple life. Detach from everything that you own.
