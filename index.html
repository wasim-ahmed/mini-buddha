-If you keep talking to someone else in your mind then it is called as overthinking. Now the truth is we indulge in overthinking often when we are afraid of confronting a situation, see this is how our mind plays with us. It let us talk away from situation but when we are present in that situation chances are it won't help!. This is the normal behaviour of an untrained mind, we just have to train our mind the same way we train our muscles to be strong.

We have to train our mind in not to indulge in overthinking, then we can face the actual scenario with more confidence. if you are talking to someone else in your mind the reason may be the other person is too adamant or ignorant, just leave it there. Symptoms

Falling Sensation
- You ever had a feeling that you are tripping or going down. you feel that that suddenly you start loosing control over your body and going to fell down but it never happens!. You are always right there standing on your feet just feeling uncomfortable and nervous. Sounds familiar ! you ever had that, may be you are living in such a situation day after day and wondering what to do, as doctor already said that you are ok all of your medical tests are fine but you only know that what you are going through.Chance are people don't understand beacause to them you seems just fine as you have not fractured your bone which they can see and offer their sympathy Remember this is different situation from vertigo because in vertigo your surroundings move and you stand still which is contrary in this case as here surroundings are absolute stationary it is you who is shaking.

This happens when you are under immense stress and living in it over & over. The brain signals the body that this situation is beyond your control and it is better to shutdown. This might happen more intensly when you are out in public and as you come home you feel relaxed.This is just the outcome of what your mind feels. This is the falling sensation when you are awake.This is often termed as social anxiety too or as a software error of human body.
Heartbeat Palpitation
- Does it happened to you that sometimes your heart beat raises exponentially high as if you are going to get a cardiac arrest but you never get a heart attack you just feel nervous and after sometimes you just feel good, in this case too chances are your doctor has said you are just fine , all your reports are correct yet you only know how miserable your life has become with this issue which is not ending.
These situations are caused when there is a imbalance in our brain, there are several hormones which gets disturbed when we get through rough time or having high stress. The level of happiness hormone(dopamine & serotonin) goes down and the level of stress hormone(cortisol) goes up.
